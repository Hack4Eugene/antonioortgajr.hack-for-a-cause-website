Conduct

All Hackathon attendees must adhere to a code of conduct while on the premises as follows: Be considerate of your fellow humans– do not harass, threaten, disrespect, discriminate, or otherwise annoy others.

Breaking the code of conduct may result in being asked to leave.

Additionally, smoking is prohibited inside the building.
